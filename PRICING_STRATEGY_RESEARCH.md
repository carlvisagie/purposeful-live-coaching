### 2. Behavioral Economics Insights

**Why Bundling Works Psychologically:**

1. **Reduces Decision Fatigue**
   - Too many choices reduce conversion rates by up to 10% (Source: Harvard Business Review)
   - Simplifies purchasing process
   - Customers prefer "done-for-you" packages

2. **Perceived Savings Effect**
   - Bundles create perception of getting a "deal"
   - Anchoring effect: Bundle price looks cheaper than sum of individual items
   - Increases willingness to pay

3. **Commitment & Completion**
   - Bundled programs have **higher completion rates** because customers feel they've made a bigger investment
   - Sunk cost fallacy works in your favor - customers use what they paid for

**Why √Ä La Carte Can Fail:**

1. **Choice Paralysis**
   - 31 modules = overwhelming decision
   - Customers may buy nothing instead of choosing

2. **Lower Perceived Value**
   - Individual modules seem "small" and less transformative
   - Harder to justify price per module

3. **Lower Engagement**
   - Customers cherry-pick easiest modules, skip harder ones
   - Miss the holistic transformation journey

**Proposed Structure:**

- Private community access

- **Goal:** High-ticket clients seeking complete transformation
