# Competitor Feature Analysis

## WYSA Features (from wysa.com)

### Core Features:
1. **24/7 AI Chatbot** - Always available, anonymous, private, secure
2. **Non-judgmental safe space** - Share thoughts without fear of judgment
3. **Emotional support anytime** - Day or night support

### Self-Care Library:
- **200+ digital tools** for mental health and wellbeing
- Guided meditations
- Breathing exercises
- CBT-based techniques
- Tools for: work stress, breakups, personal challenges

### Human Coaching:
- **4 live text-based sessions per month** with qualified coaches
- **Support between sessions** - message coach anytime
- In-app text-based chat sessions

### Crisis Support:
- **SOS feature** - One tap access to crisis resources
- Grounding techniques
- Emergency contacts
- AI screens for signs of distress
- Escalation to local/national crisis helplines

### Key Differentiators:
- Clinically validated (research studies)
- CBT and DBT based
- Rule-based AI + LLM hybrid
- Focus on accessibility
