## 4. Content Marketing Strategy

Content is the foundation of our marketing. We will create high-value, evidence-based content that establishes Purposeful Live Coaching as a trusted authority in the wellness space.

**Content Pillars:**

1.  **Mental & Emotional Wellness:** Stress management, mindfulness, resilience, positive psychology.
2.  **Physical Performance:** Nutrition, sleep optimization, fitness, biohacking.
3.  **Personal & Professional Growth:** Productivity, leadership, career development, purpose.
4.  **Specialized Support:** Autism, ADHD, relationships, financial wellness.

**Content Funnel:**

| Funnel Stage | Content Type | Goal |
|---|---|---|
| **Top of Funnel (Awareness)** | - Blog posts (e.g., "10 Ways to Reduce Stress")<br>- Social media tips<br>- Infographics | Attract new visitors, build brand awareness |
| **Middle of Funnel (Consideration)** | - In-depth guides (e.g., "The Ultimate Guide to Sleep Optimization")<br>- Webinars<br>- Email courses<br>- Lead magnets (checklists, templates) | Capture leads, build trust and authority |
| **Bottom of Funnel (Conversion)** | - Case studies & testimonials<br>- Product demos & tutorials<br>- Free trial offer<br>- Pricing page | Convert leads into paying customers |

**Blog Strategy (to be executed by Penn & Seomi):**

-   **Frequency:** 2 new blog posts per week (1,500+ words each).
-   **Topics:** Address common pain points and questions of our target audience.
-   **SEO:** Each post will be optimized for a primary keyword.
-   **Call to Action (CTA):** Each post will include a CTA to download a related lead magnet or sign up for the free trial.

## 6. Email Marketing Strategy

Email is our primary channel for nurturing leads and converting them into customers.

**Email Sequences (to be executed by Emmie):**

1.  **Welcome Series (5 emails):** For new subscribers. Introduces the brand, provides value, and encourages trial signup.
2.  **Trial Onboarding (7 emails):** For new trial users. Guides them through the platform and highlights key features.
3.  **Nurture Sequence (ongoing):** For subscribers who haven't converted. Provides ongoing value and soft-sells the subscription.
4.  **Weekly Newsletter:** For all subscribers. Shares the latest blog posts, tips, and platform updates.
