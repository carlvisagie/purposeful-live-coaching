# Refund Policy

**Last Updated:** December 3, 2024

**Effective Date:** December 3, 2024

---

## Overview

At PurposefulLive, we strive to provide exceptional AI-powered coaching services. This Refund Policy explains our approach to refunds and cancellations for our subscription-based services.

---

## 7-Day Free Trial

All new subscribers receive a **7-day free trial** period. During this trial:

- You have **full access** to all features of your selected tier
- You will **not be charged** during the trial period
- You can **cancel anytime** during the trial with no charges
- If you do not cancel before the trial ends, your subscription will automatically convert to a paid subscription

**No refunds are necessary for free trial cancellations** since no payment has been processed.

---

## Monthly Subscriptions

### Cancellation Policy

Monthly subscriptions can be canceled at any time through your account dashboard. When you cancel:

- Your access **continues until the end of your current billing period**
- You will **not be charged** for subsequent months
- **No refunds** are provided for the current billing period
- You can **reactivate** your subscription at any time

### No Refunds for Partial Months

We do not provide refunds for partial months of service. If you cancel on day 15 of your billing cycle, you will retain access for the remaining 15 days, but no refund will be issued for unused time.

### Example

- You subscribe on January 1st ($29/month)
- You cancel on January 15th
- You retain access until January 31st
- No refund is issued
- You are not charged on February 1st

---

## Yearly Subscriptions

### 30-Day Money-Back Guarantee

Yearly subscriptions include a **30-day money-back guarantee**. Within the first 30 days of your yearly subscription:

- You can request a **full refund** for any reason
- Simply contact us at support@purposefullive.com
- Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days
- Access is terminated upon refund

### After 30 Days

After the 30-day guarantee period:

- **Pro-rated refunds** may be provided at our discretion
- Refunds are calculated based on unused months remaining
- Human coaching sessions already used are deducted from refund amount
- Contact support@purposefullive.com to request a refund

### Example

- You purchase a yearly AI-Only subscription on January 1st ($290/year)
- You request a refund on March 1st (2 months used, 10 months remaining)
- Pro-rated refund: $290 Ã— (10/12) = $241.67
- Refund is processed within 5-7 business days

---

## Human Coaching Sessions

### Non-Refundable Once Used

Human coaching sessions included in Hybrid and Premium tiers are **non-refundable once used**:

- If you complete a human coaching session, that session cannot be refunded
- Unused human sessions may be refunded as part of a yearly subscription refund
- Human sessions do not roll over month-to-month on monthly subscriptions
- Human sessions do not roll over if you downgrade your tier

### Cancellation Before Session

If you book a human coaching session but cancel **at least 24 hours in advance**:

- The session is returned to your account
- You can rebook at a later time
- No penalty is applied

If you cancel **less than 24 hours before** a scheduled session:

- The session is considered used
- No refund or credit is provided
- This policy ensures coaches are compensated for their reserved time

---

## Payment Failures

### Automatic Retry

If your payment method fails:

- We will **automatically retry** the payment 3 times over 2 weeks
- You will receive **email notifications** after each failed attempt
- Your access **continues** during the retry period

### Access Suspension

If all payment retries fail:

- Your access will be **suspended** after the final failed attempt
- Your data and conversation history are **preserved** for 60 days
- You can **reactivate** by updating your payment method

### No Refunds for Failed Payments

If your subscription is suspended due to payment failure:

- No refunds are provided for the period of suspension
- You are responsible for updating your payment information
- Access is restored immediately upon successful payment

---

## Exceptions and Special Circumstances

### Technical Issues

If you experience significant technical issues that prevent you from using our service:

- Contact us immediately at support@purposefullive.com
- We will work to resolve the issue promptly
- If we cannot resolve the issue within 7 days, we may provide a pro-rated refund at our discretion

### Service Outages

If our service experiences an outage lasting more than 24 hours:

- Your subscription will be **automatically extended** by the duration of the outage
- No refund is necessary as you receive additional time
- We will notify you of the extension via email

### Fraudulent Activity

If we detect fraudulent activity on your account:

- Your account will be immediately suspended
- No refunds will be provided
- We reserve the right to pursue legal action

## How to Request a Refund

### Step 1: Contact Support

Email us at **support@purposefullive.com** with:

- Your account email address
- Reason for refund request
- Any relevant details or concerns

### Step 2: Review Process

We will review your request within **2 business days** and respond with:

- Approval or denial of refund
- Refund amount (if applicable)
- Expected processing timeline

### Step 3: Processing

Approved refunds are processed within **5-7 business days**:

- Refunds are issued to your original payment method
- You will receive a confirmation email
- Your access will be terminated upon refund

## Subscription Modifications

### Upgrades

If you upgrade from a lower tier to a higher tier:

- You are charged the **pro-rated difference** immediately
- Your billing date remains the same
- No refunds are provided if you later downgrade

### Downgrades

If you downgrade from a higher tier to a lower tier:

- The change takes effect at your **next billing date**
- You retain access to your current tier until then
- **No refunds** are provided for the difference in price

### Example

- You have AI-Only ($29/month) and upgrade to Hybrid ($149/month) on day 15
- You pay $60 immediately (half of $120 difference)
- Your next bill on day 1 of next month is $149
- If you downgrade back to AI-Only on day 20, change takes effect on day 1 of next month
- No refund for the $60 upgrade charge

## Dispute Resolution

### Chargebacks

If you initiate a chargeback with your credit card company:

- Your account will be immediately suspended
- We will provide evidence of service delivery to your card issuer
- If the chargeback is resolved in our favor, your account remains suspended
- If the chargeback is resolved in your favor, your account is terminated

**Please contact us directly before initiating a chargeback.** We are committed to resolving any issues fairly and promptly.

### Mediation

If you are not satisfied with our refund decision:

- You may request mediation through a neutral third party
- Both parties agree to participate in good faith
- Mediation costs are split equally
