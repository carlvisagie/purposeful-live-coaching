# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** December 3, 2025  
**Last Updated:** December 3, 2025

## Introduction

Welcome to Purposeful Live Coaching ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal information with care and transparency. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our AI-powered coaching platform.

**Important:** This platform provides wellness coaching and emotional support through AI technology. It is NOT a medical service and is NOT covered by HIPAA (Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act). Your conversations and data are protected by this Privacy Policy, but not by medical privacy laws.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Account Information
When you create an account, we collect:
- Name
- Email address
- Password (encrypted)
- Date of birth (for age verification - must be 18+)
- Profile information you choose to provide

### 2. Conversation Data
When you use our AI coaching service, we collect:
- All messages you send to the AI coach
- AI responses
- Conversation timestamps
- Conversation ratings and feedback
- Crisis detection flags (if triggered)

### 3. Emotional Wellness Data
We collect data you voluntarily provide:
- Emotion logs and mood tracking
- Journal entries
- Coping strategies you save
- Progress notes

### 4. Files You Upload
- Audio recordings (voice memos, session recordings)
- Video files
- Documents
- Images
- Transcriptions of audio/video files

### 5. Usage Information
- Pages you visit on our platform
- Features you use
- Time spent on platform
- Device information (browser type, operating system)
- IP address

### 6. Payment Information
- Payment method details (processed securely by Stripe)
- Billing address
- Transaction history
- Subscription status

We do NOT store your full credit card numbers. Payment processing is handled by Stripe, our secure payment processor.

## How We Use Your Information

### 1. To Provide Our Services
- Deliver AI coaching conversations
- Track your progress and emotional wellness
- Provide personalized recommendations
- Manage your subscription and billing

### 2. To Improve Our Services
- Analyze conversation quality and AI performance
- Identify patterns to improve AI responses
- Develop new features and improvements
- Monitor for safety and compliance issues

### 3. For Safety and Crisis Detection
- Detect expressions of suicidal ideation or self-harm
- Alert human coaches when crisis situations are detected
- Track crisis referrals (required by California law)
- Ensure user safety and well-being

### 4. To Communicate With You
- Send subscription confirmations and receipts
- Notify you of important account changes
- Send trial expiration reminders
- Provide customer support
- Send usage summaries (if you opt in)

### 5. For Legal Compliance
- Comply with California and New York AI companion laws
- Respond to legal requests and prevent fraud
- Enforce our Terms of Service
- Protect our rights and property

## How We Share Your Information

### We Do NOT Sell Your Data
We will never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

### Limited Sharing With Service Providers
We share your information only with trusted service providers who help us operate our platform:

1. **Stripe** - Payment processing (PCI-DSS compliant)
2. **OpenAI** - AI conversation processing (your messages are sent to OpenAI's API to generate responses)
3. **Cloud Storage** - File storage for uploads (AWS S3)
4. **Email Service** - Transactional emails

These providers are contractually obligated to protect your data and use it only for the services they provide to us.

### Sharing With Human Coaches
If you subscribe to a tier that includes human coaching sessions:
- Your AI conversation history is accessible to your assigned coach
- This allows coaches to provide better, more informed support
- Coaches are bound by confidentiality agreements

### Legal Requirements
We may disclose your information if required by law:
- In response to valid legal process (subpoena, court order)
- To protect our rights or property
- To prevent fraud or illegal activity
- In emergency situations to protect someone's safety

### Crisis Situations
If our AI detects a crisis situation (suicidal ideation, self-harm), we will:
- Automatically notify the platform owner/admin
- Provide crisis resources (988 Lifeline)
- May contact emergency services if we believe there is imminent danger

## How We Protect Your Information

### Security Measures
- All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
- Database is encrypted at rest
- Passwords are hashed and salted
- Access controls limit who can view your data
- Regular security audits and updates

### Data Storage
- Data is stored on secure cloud servers
- Automatic backups to prevent data loss
- Geographic redundancy for reliability

### Access Controls
- Only authorized personnel can access user data
- Access is logged and monitored
- Multi-factor authentication for admin access

## Your Rights and Choices

### Access Your Data
You can access all your data through your account dashboard:
- View all conversations
- Download your conversation history
- View emotion logs and journal entries
- Access uploaded files

### Delete Your Data
You have the right to delete your information:
- Delete individual conversations
- Delete uploaded files
- Request full account deletion (contact us at [support email])

**Note:** Some data may be retained for legal compliance (e.g., payment records for tax purposes) even after account deletion.

### Export Your Data
You can export your data in common formats:
- Conversation transcripts (text format)
- Emotion logs (CSV format)
- Journal entries (text format)

Contact us to request a full data export.

### Opt Out of Communications
You can control what emails you receive:
- Opt out of usage summaries
- You cannot opt out of essential service emails (receipts, security alerts)

### Correct Your Information
You can update your account information anytime:
- Change your name or email
- Update profile information
- Correct inaccurate data

## Data Retention

### Active Accounts
We retain your data as long as your account is active:
- Conversation history: Retained indefinitely (unless you delete)
- Emotion logs: Retained indefinitely (unless you delete)
- Files: Retained until you delete them

### Deleted Accounts
After you delete your account:
- Most data is deleted within 30 days
- Some data is retained for legal compliance:
  - Payment records: 7 years (tax law requirement)
  - Crisis incident reports: 7 years (legal protection)
  - Aggregated analytics (anonymized): Indefinitely

### Inactive Accounts
If you don't log in for 2 years:
- We may send a reminder email
- After 3 years of inactivity, we may delete your account
- You'll receive 30 days notice before deletion

## Children's Privacy

Our service is NOT intended for users under 18 years old.

- We require users to be 18+ during signup
- We verify age through birthdate collection
- If we discover a user is under 18, we will delete their account
- We do not knowingly collect data from minors

If you believe a minor has created an account, please contact us immediately.

## California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

### Right to Know
You can request:
- What personal information we collect
- How we use it
- Who we share it with
- How long we retain it

### Right to Delete
You can request deletion of your personal information (with some exceptions for legal compliance).

### Right to Opt-Out
You have the right to opt-out of the "sale" of your personal information. **We do not sell your information.**

### Right to Non-Discrimination
We will not discriminate against you for exercising your privacy rights.

### How to Exercise Your Rights
Contact us with your request. We will respond within 45 days.

## Specific Disclosures

### California AI Companion Law Compliance
As required by California law, we disclose:
- **Crisis detection methodology:** Keyword-based detection with multi-level risk assessment using evidence-based mental health research
- **Crisis referral tracking:** We track how many times users are referred to crisis services (988 Lifeline, emergency services)
- **Annual reporting:** We will report crisis statistics to California Office of Suicide Prevention starting July 2027

### New York AI Companion Law Compliance
As required by New York law:
- We clearly disclose that you are interacting with AI, not a human
- This disclosure appears at the start of conversations and every 3 hours
- We have protocols to detect and respond to expressions of self-harm
