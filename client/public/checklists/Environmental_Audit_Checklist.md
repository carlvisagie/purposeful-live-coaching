# Environmental Audit Checklist

**Module:** Health & Wellness

**Purpose:** To help you to assess your home and work environments for potential health hazards.

**Instructions:** Use this checklist to audit your home and work environments. Identify any potential health hazards and make a plan to address them.

---

### Home Environment

*   [ ] **Air Quality:** Is the air in your home clean and fresh? Use an air purifier to remove dust, pollen, and other allergens. Open your windows regularly to allow fresh air to circulate.
*   [ ] **Water Quality:** Is the water in your home clean and safe to drink? Use a water filter to remove chlorine, fluoride, and other contaminants.
*   [ ] **Light Exposure:** Are you getting enough natural light during the day? Are you avoiding blue light from screens before bed?
*   [ ] **Noise Levels:** Is your home a quiet and peaceful place to be? Use earplugs or a white noise machine to block out noise.
*   [ ] **Clutter:** Is your home free of clutter? A cluttered home can be a source of stress and anxiety.
*   [ ] **Toxins:** Are you using natural and organic cleaning products and personal care products? Are you avoiding pesticides and herbicides?

### Work Environment

*   [ ] **Ergonomics:** Is your workstation set up ergonomically? Make sure your chair, desk, and computer are all at the proper height.
*   [ ] **Air Quality:** Is the air in your workplace clean and fresh? If not, talk to your employer about getting an air purifier.
*   [ ] **Lighting:** Is the lighting in your workplace adequate? Poor lighting can cause eye strain and headaches.
*   [ ] **Noise Levels:** Is your workplace a quiet and productive place to be? If not, talk to your employer about getting noise-cancelling headphones.
*   [ ] **Breaks:** Are you taking regular breaks throughout the day? Get up and move around every 30-60 minutes to avoid sitting for long periods of time.
