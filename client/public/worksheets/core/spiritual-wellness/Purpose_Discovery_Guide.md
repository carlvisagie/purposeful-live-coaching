# Purpose Discovery Guide

**Module:** Spiritual Wellness

**Purpose:** To help you to discover your unique purpose and to create a life that is aligned with it.

**Instructions:** This guide contains a series of reflective questions to help you to explore your passions, skills, and values. Be honest and open with yourself as you answer these questions. There are no right or wrong answers.

---

### Part 1: Your Passions

*What do you love to do? What makes you feel alive and energized?*

1.  What activities make you lose track of time?



2.  What topics do you love to learn about?



3.  What would you do if you knew you could not fail?



### Part 2: Your Skills

*What are you good at? What are your natural talents and developed abilities?*

1.  What skills have you developed through your education, work, and life experience?



2.  What do people often come to you for help with?



3.  What are you most proud of accomplishing?



### Part 3: Your Values

*What is most important to you in life? What are your guiding principles?*

1.  What are your top 5 core values? (Refer to the Values Clarification Worksheet if needed.)



2.  What causes or issues do you care about deeply?



3.  What kind of impact do you want to have on the world?



### Part 4: Your Purpose Statement

*Based on your reflections above, craft a purpose statement that captures your unique contribution to the world. Your purpose statement should be a living document that you can revise and refine over time.*

**My purpose is to use my passion for __________________ and my skills in __________________ to __________________ so that __________________.**

---

### Living Your Purpose

*Your purpose is not just a statement; it is a way of life. How can you live in greater alignment with your purpose?*

**What is one small action I can take this week to live my purpose?**
