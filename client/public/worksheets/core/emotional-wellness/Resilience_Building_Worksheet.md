# Resilience Building Worksheet

**Module:** Emotional Wellness

**Purpose:** To identify and strengthen your personal sources of resilience.

**Instructions:** Complete the following exercises to build your resilience toolkit. Revisit this worksheet regularly to track your progress and add new strategies.

---

### Part 2: Cultivating a Growth Mindset

*A growth mindset is the belief that your abilities can be developed through dedication and hard work. It is a key component of resilience.*

**1. Identify a recent setback or failure.**



**2. What did you learn from this experience?**



**3. How can you apply this learning to future challenges?**



### Part 3: Nurturing Supportive Relationships

*Strong social connections are a powerful buffer against stress and adversity.*

**1. List three people in your life who you can turn to for support.**

    1.  __________________
    2.  __________________
    3.  __________________

**2. What is one thing you can do this week to strengthen one of these relationships?**

*   *e.g., send a thoughtful text, schedule a phone call, plan a coffee date.*



### Part 4: Practicing Self-Compassion

*Self-compassion involves treating yourself with the same kindness and understanding that you would offer to a good friend.*

**1. Think of a recent mistake you made. What did you say to yourself in that moment?**



**2. Now, imagine a close friend made the same mistake. What would you say to them?**



**3. Write a compassionate message to yourself about the mistake.**



### Part 5: Finding Meaning and Purpose

*A sense of purpose can provide a source of strength and motivation during difficult times.*

**1. What activities make you feel most alive and engaged?**



**2. How can you incorporate more of these activities into your life?**



**3. What is one small way you can contribute to something larger than yourself this week?**

*   *e.g., volunteer, help a neighbor, donate to a cause you care about.*
