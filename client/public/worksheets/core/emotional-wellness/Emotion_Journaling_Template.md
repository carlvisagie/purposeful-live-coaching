# Emotion Journaling Template

**Module:** Emotional Wellness

**Purpose:** To build emotional awareness and identify patterns in your emotional life.

**Instructions:** Use this template daily to check in with your emotions. Be honest and non-judgmental with yourself. The goal is simply to notice and name your feelings.

---

### Daily Emotion Journal

**Date:** __________________

**Time:** __________________

**1. What am I feeling right now?**

*   *Identify and name the primary emotion(s) you are experiencing. Use the Emotion Wheel below for inspiration if needed.*



**2. What physical sensations do I notice in my body?**

*   *e.g., tightness in my chest, butterflies in my stomach, warmth in my face, relaxed shoulders.*



**3. What thoughts are going through my mind?**

*   *Write down any thoughts or beliefs that are present, without judgment.*



**4. What happened just before I started feeling this way?**

*   *Identify any triggers or events that may have contributed to your current emotional state.*



**5. How intense is this feeling on a scale of 1-10?**

*   *(1 = very mild, 10 = very intense)*



**6. What do I need right now?**

*   *Consider what would be most supportive for you in this moment. e.g., a hug, a walk, a glass of water, a few deep breaths.*

### Weekly Reflection

*At the end of each week, review your daily entries and reflect on the following questions:*

**1. What patterns do I notice in my emotions?**



**2. What are my most common emotional triggers?**



**3. What situations or activities tend to bring me joy and contentment?**



**4. What have I learned about myself this week?**
