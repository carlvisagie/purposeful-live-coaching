# Emotional Triggers Tracker

**Module:** Emotional Wellness

**Purpose:** To identify the situations, people, and thoughts that trigger strong emotional responses.

**Instructions:** Use this tracker to record your emotional triggers as they happen. Over time, you will begin to see patterns that can help you to better understand and manage your emotions.

### Reflection Questions

*At the end of each week, review your log and reflect on the following questions:*

**1. What are my most common emotional triggers?**



**2. Are there any specific people, places, or situations that consistently trigger strong emotions?**



**3. What are my most common automatic thoughts when I am triggered?**



**4. How do my emotional responses affect my behavior and my relationships?**



**5. What is one thing I can do differently next time I am faced with one of my common triggers?**
