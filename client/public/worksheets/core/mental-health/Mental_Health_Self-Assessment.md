# Mental Health Self-Assessment

**Module:** Mental Health

**Purpose:** To provide a snapshot of your current mental well-being and to identify areas for growth.

**Instructions:** This is not a diagnostic tool. It is a self-assessment to help you to become more aware of your mental health. Answer the following questions honestly and without judgment. Use your responses to guide your learning and to track your progress over time.

---

### Scoring

*Add up your scores for all 15 questions.*

*   **15-30:** You may be experiencing significant challenges to your mental well-being. It may be helpful to seek support from a mental health professional.
*   **31-60:** You may be experiencing some challenges to your mental well-being. This is a good time to focus on self-care and to develop healthy coping strategies.
*   **61-75:** You are likely experiencing a good level of mental well-being. Continue to practice self-care and to nurture your mental health.
