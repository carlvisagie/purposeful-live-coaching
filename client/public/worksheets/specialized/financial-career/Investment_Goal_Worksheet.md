### Step 3: Choose Your Investment Strategy

*What is your investment strategy? Your strategy should be based on your goals, your risk tolerance, and your time horizon.*

*   **Conservative:** A conservative strategy focuses on preserving capital and generating a modest return. This strategy is appropriate for investors with a low risk tolerance and a short time horizon.
*   **Moderate:** A moderate strategy seeks to balance growth and preservation of capital. This strategy is appropriate for investors with a moderate risk tolerance and a medium time horizon.
*   **Aggressive:** An aggressive strategy focuses on maximizing growth and is willing to take on a significant amount of risk. This strategy is appropriate for investors with a high risk tolerance and a long time horizon.
