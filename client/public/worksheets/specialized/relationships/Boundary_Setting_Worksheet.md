### Part 1: Understanding Boundaries

*Boundaries are the limits we set for ourselves in relationships. They are a way of protecting our physical, emotional, and mental well-being. There are many different types of boundaries, including:*

*   **Physical Boundaries:** Your personal space, privacy, and body.
*   **Emotional Boundaries:** Your feelings and emotions.
*   **Mental Boundaries:** Your thoughts, beliefs, and opinions.
*   **Time Boundaries:** Your time and energy.
*   **Financial Boundaries:** Your money and possessions.
