**Material Name:** Weekly Reflection Questions
**Category:** Worksheet

## Purpose and Instructions

This **Weekly Reflection Worksheet** is a structured, evidence-based tool designed to help you, the coaching client, consolidate learning, celebrate successes, and proactively plan for the week ahead. Consistent reflection is a cornerstone of continuous personal and professional development, transforming experiences into actionable insights [1].

### How to Use This Worksheet

1.  **Schedule Time:** Dedicate 30-60 minutes at the end of your work week (e.g., Friday afternoon) or the start of your personal week (e.g., Sunday evening).
2.  **Be Honest:** Answer all questions with complete honesty and without self-judgment. The goal is clarity, not perfection.
3.  **Focus on Action:** The final section is critical. Translate your reflections into 1-3 concrete, measurable actions for the upcoming week.
