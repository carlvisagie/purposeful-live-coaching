# Healthy Recipe Collection

**Module:** Nutrition

**Purpose:** To provide a collection of healthy and delicious recipes that are easy to make.

**Instructions:** This collection includes recipes for breakfast, lunch, dinner, and snacks. All of the recipes are made with whole, unprocessed foods and are packed with nutrients.

---

### Breakfast

**1. Oatmeal with Berries and Nuts**

*   **Ingredients:** 1/2 cup rolled oats, 1 cup water or milk, 1/2 cup mixed berries, 1/4 cup chopped nuts
*   **Instructions:** Bring the water or milk to a boil in a small saucepan. Add the oats and cook for 5-7 minutes, or until the oats are cooked through. Top with the berries and nuts.

**2. Scrambled Eggs with Spinach and Avocado**

*   **Ingredients:** 2 eggs, 1 cup spinach, 1/2 avocado, 1 tsp olive oil
*   **Instructions:** Heat the olive oil in a small skillet over medium heat. Add the spinach and cook until wilted. In a small bowl, whisk the eggs. Add the eggs to the skillet and cook, stirring occasionally, until the eggs are cooked through. Top with the avocado.

### Lunch

**1. Quinoa Salad with Roasted Vegetables**

*   **Ingredients:** 1 cup cooked quinoa, 2 cups roasted vegetables (e.g., broccoli, carrots, bell peppers), 1/4 cup chopped parsley, 2 tbsp olive oil, 1 tbsp lemon juice
*   **Instructions:** In a large bowl, combine the quinoa, roasted vegetables, and parsley. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil and lemon juice. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss to combine.

**2. Lentil Soup**

*   **Ingredients:** 1 tbsp olive oil, 1 onion, chopped, 2 carrots, chopped, 2 celery stalks, chopped, 1 cup brown or green lentils, 4 cups vegetable broth, 1 tsp dried thyme
*   **Instructions:** Heat the olive oil in a large pot or Dutch oven over medium heat. Add the onion, carrots, and celery and cook until softened. Add the lentils, vegetable broth, and thyme. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat and simmer for 30-40 minutes, or until the lentils are cooked through.

### Dinner

**1. Baked Salmon with Roasted Asparagus**

*   **Ingredients:** 1 salmon fillet, 1 bunch asparagus, 1 tbsp olive oil, 1/2 tsp salt, 1/4 tsp black pepper
*   **Instructions:** Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C). Place the salmon and asparagus on a baking sheet. Drizzle with the olive oil and season with the salt and pepper. Bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the salmon is cooked through.

**2. Chicken Stir-Fry**

*   **Ingredients:** 1 lb boneless, skinless chicken breast, cut into bite-sized pieces, 4 cups mixed vegetables (e.g., broccoli, carrots, bell peppers, snow peas), 1/4 cup soy sauce, 2 tbsp honey, 1 tbsp cornstarch, 1 tbsp sesame oil
*   **Instructions:** In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, honey, and cornstarch. Heat the sesame oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add the chicken and cook until browned. Add the vegetables and cook until tender-crisp. Add the sauce and cook until thickened.
