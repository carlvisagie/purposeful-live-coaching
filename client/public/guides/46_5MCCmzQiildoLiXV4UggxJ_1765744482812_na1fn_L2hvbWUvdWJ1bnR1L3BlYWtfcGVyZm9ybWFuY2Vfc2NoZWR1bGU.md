# Peak Performance Schedule: Optimizing Your Day for Maximum Impact

**Category: Worksheet**

## Purpose and Instructions

This worksheet is designed to help you move beyond simple time management and create a schedule that aligns with your natural biological rhythms, leading to higher quality work, sustained energy, and reduced stress. This is an **evidence-based** approach to productivity, leveraging principles of chronobiology and ultradian cycles [1] [2].

### Instructions for Use

1.  **Complete the Chronotype Assessment** in Section 1 to identify your natural energy pattern.
2.  **Analyze Your Current Week** and identify your 3 most demanding tasks (Deep Work).
3.  **Map Your Schedule** in Section 2, aligning your Deep Work blocks with your identified peak energy times.
4.  **Implement and Reflect** using the questions and action steps in Section 3.
