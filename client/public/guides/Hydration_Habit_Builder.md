# Hydration Habit Builder

**Module:** Nutrition

**Purpose:** To help you to build a habit of drinking enough water each day.

**Instructions:** This guide provides tips and tricks for staying hydrated. Use this guide to create a plan for building a hydration habit that works for you.

---

### Why is Hydration Important?

Water is essential for life. It makes up about 60% of your body weight and is involved in many important bodily functions, including:

*   Regulating body temperature
*   Transporting nutrients and oxygen to your cells
*   Flushing out waste products
*   Lubricating your joints
*   Protecting your organs and tissues

### How Much Water Do You Need?

The amount of water you need each day depends on a variety of factors, including your age, activity level, and the climate you live in. A general rule of thumb is to drink eight 8-ounce glasses of water per day, which is about 2 liters, or half a gallon.

### Tips for Staying Hydrated

*   **Carry a water bottle with you:** This will make it easy to drink water throughout the day.
*   **Set reminders:** Set reminders on your phone or computer to drink water at regular intervals.
*   **Drink a glass of water before each meal:** This will help you to stay hydrated and may also help you to eat less.
*   **Add flavor to your water:** If you don't like the taste of plain water, try adding a slice of lemon, lime, or cucumber.
*   **Eat your water:** Many fruits and vegetables are high in water content, such as watermelon, strawberries, and celery.
*   **Track your intake:** Use a water tracking app or a simple tally sheet to track your water intake each day.
