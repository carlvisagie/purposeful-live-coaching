# Toxin Swap Guide

**Module:** Health & Wellness

**Purpose:** To provide a guide to reducing your exposure to toxins in your home and personal care products.

**Instructions:** This guide provides a list of common toxins and their healthier alternatives. Use this guide to make small, sustainable changes to reduce your toxic load.

---

### Common Toxins and Their Healthier Alternatives

| Category | Toxin | Healthier Alternative |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Cleaning Products** | Bleach, ammonia, phthalates | Vinegar, baking soda, essential oils |
| **Personal Care Products** | Parabens, phthalates, synthetic fragrances | Natural and organic personal care products |
| **Cookware** | Teflon, aluminum | Cast iron, stainless steel, ceramic |
| **Food Storage** | Plastic | Glass, stainless steel, silicone |
| **Water** | Chlorine, fluoride, heavy metals | Filtered water |

### Tips for Reducing Your Toxic Load

*   **Read labels:** Be aware of the ingredients in your food, cleaning products, and personal care products.
*   **Choose natural and organic products:** Whenever possible, choose products that are made with natural and organic ingredients.
*   **Ventilate your home:** Open your windows regularly to allow fresh air to circulate.
*   **Use a water filter:** A water filter can help to remove chlorine, fluoride, and other contaminants from your drinking water.
*   **Avoid using pesticides and herbicides:** These chemicals can be harmful to your health and the environment.
