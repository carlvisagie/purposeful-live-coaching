# Exercise Form Checklist

**Module:** Physical Fitness

**Purpose:** To provide a checklist for maintaining proper form during fundamental exercises.

**Instructions:** Use this checklist to review your form before and during your workouts. You can also use it to give feedback to a workout partner.

---

### The Squat

*   [ ] Feet shoulder-width apart, toes slightly out
*   [ ] Core engaged, chest up
*   [ ] Hips initiate the movement
*   [ ] Knees track over toes
*   [ ] Hips go parallel to or below knees
*   [ ] Drive through heels to stand up

### The Deadlift

*   [ ] Feet hip-width apart, mid-foot under the bar
*   [ ] Grip just outside shins
*   [ ] Back straight, chest up
*   [ ] Hips higher than knees
*   [ ] Drive through heels, extend hips and knees
*   [ ] Lower the bar with control

### The Push-Up

*   [ ] Hands slightly wider than shoulder-width
*   [ ] Body in a straight line from head to heels
*   [ ] Core and glutes engaged
*   [ ] Elbows at a 45-degree angle
*   [ ] Chest just above the floor at the bottom
*   [ ] Push through hands to return to start

### The Bent-Over Row

*   [ ] Feet shoulder-width apart, knees slightly bent
*   [ ] Hinge at hips, back straight
*   [ ] Grip slightly wider than shoulder-width
*   [ ] Pull bar to lower chest
*   [ ] Squeeze shoulder blades together
*   [ ] Lower the bar with control
