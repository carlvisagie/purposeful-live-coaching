# Fear Setting Worksheet: Overcoming Paralysis by Analysis

**Material Name:** Fear Setting Worksheet
**Category:** Worksheet
**Author:** Manus AI

## Purpose and Instructions

This **Fear Setting Worksheet** is a structured, evidence-based tool designed to help you overcome the paralysis that often accompanies major life or business decisions. Inspired by the methodology popularized by entrepreneur and author Tim Ferriss, this exercise is not about being fearless, but about defining your fears with precision to minimize their power and maximize your potential for action [1].

The process involves three distinct pages, or phases:
1.  **Define:** Clearly articulate the worst-case scenarios (the "What Ifs").
2.  **Prevent & Repair:** Develop concrete steps to prevent the worst outcomes and repair them if they occur.
3.  **Benefit:** Analyze the cost of inaction and the potential benefits of even a partial success.

**Instructions:** Dedicate 30-60 minutes of uninterrupted time. Be brutally honest and specific in your answers. Use the provided spaces and tables for structured reflection.
