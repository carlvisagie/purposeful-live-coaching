**Material Name:** Progress Tracking System

## I. Purpose and Instructions

### Purpose
This **Progress Tracking System** is a structured worksheet designed to help you systematically monitor your progress, maintain accountability, and make data-driven adjustments to your action plan. Consistent tracking is an evidence-based practice that significantly increases the likelihood of achieving long-term goals [1].

### Instructions for Use
1.  **Define Your Goal:** Clearly articulate your primary goal and the specific Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) you will use to measure success.
2.  **Initial Assessment:** Complete the "Starting Point" section to establish a baseline.
3.  **Weekly/Monthly Tracking:** Use the **Progress Log Table** to record your actions, the outcome, and your current status. Be honest and objective.
4.  **Reflect and Adjust:** Use the **Reflection Questions** section to analyze your data and identify what is working and what needs to change.
5.  **Plan Next Steps:** Based on your reflection, define clear, actionable steps for the next tracking period.
