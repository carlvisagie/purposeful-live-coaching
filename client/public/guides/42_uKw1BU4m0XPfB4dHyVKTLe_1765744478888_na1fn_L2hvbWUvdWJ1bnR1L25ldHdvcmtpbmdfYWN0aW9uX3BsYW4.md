## Purpose and Instructions

This **Networking Action Plan** is a structured worksheet designed to help you move beyond random interactions to build a strategic, supportive, and mutually beneficial professional network. Evidence suggests that a strong, diverse network is critical for career advancement, access to new opportunities, and personal development [1].

**Instructions:**
1.  Complete the self-assessment in Section 1 to establish a baseline.
2.  Define your clear, measurable goals in Section 2.
3.  Develop your outreach strategy and identify key contacts in Section 3.
4.  Use the tracking table in Section 4 to monitor your progress and reflect on your learning.
