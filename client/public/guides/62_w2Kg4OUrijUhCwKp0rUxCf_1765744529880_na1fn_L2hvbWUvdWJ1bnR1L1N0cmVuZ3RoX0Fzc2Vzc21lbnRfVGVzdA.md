# Strength Assessment Test: Unlocking Your Potential

## Purpose and Rationale

This Strength Assessment Test is designed to help you identify and articulate your core personal and professional strengths. Research consistently shows that focusing on and leveraging one's strengths leads to greater engagement, performance, and overall well-being [1]. By completing this assessment, you will gain clarity on your natural talents and learn how to intentionally apply them to achieve your coaching goals.

**Category:** Assessment
**Time Required:** 20-30 minutes
**Instructions:** For each statement in the inventory below, rate how accurately it describes you on a scale of 1 to 5, where 1 is "Strongly Disagree" and 5 is "Strongly Agree."
