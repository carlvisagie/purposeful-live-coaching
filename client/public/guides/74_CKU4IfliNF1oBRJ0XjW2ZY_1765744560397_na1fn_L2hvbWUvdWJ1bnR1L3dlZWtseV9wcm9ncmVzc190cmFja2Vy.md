## 1. Purpose and Instructions

This **Weekly Progress Tracker** is a critical tool designed to help you maintain momentum, gain clarity on your achievements, and proactively address challenges in your coaching journey. Consistent tracking is an evidence-based practice that enhances self-awareness and accountability, which are foundational to achieving sustainable change [1].

**Instructions:**
1.  **Complete at the end of your working week** (e.g., Friday afternoon or Saturday morning).
2.  **Be honest and objective** in your assessments.
3.  **Use the tables** to quantify your progress against your stated goals and key performance indicators.
4.  **Answer the reflection questions** thoughtfully to prepare for your next coaching session.
