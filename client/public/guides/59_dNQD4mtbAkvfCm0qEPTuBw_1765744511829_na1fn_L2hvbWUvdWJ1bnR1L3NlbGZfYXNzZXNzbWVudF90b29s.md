# Self-Assessment Tool: Professional Growth and Well-being

## 1. Purpose and Instructions

This **Self-Assessment Tool** is designed to provide a structured, evidence-based snapshot of your current state across key dimensions of professional growth and personal well-being. It is a foundational step in the coaching process, helping to identify areas of strength and opportunities for development. The insights gained will inform the creation of a targeted, effective action plan.

**Instructions:**
1.  For each statement in the assessment table below, rate your current level of agreement or satisfaction on a scale of **1 to 5**, where:
    *   **1 = Strongly Disagree / Very Dissatisfied**
    *   **5 = Strongly Agree / Highly Satisfied**
2.  Be honest and reflective in your scoring. There are no right or wrong answers.
3.  Use the "Notes" column to briefly capture any immediate thoughts or context for your score.

## 3. Scoring and Interpretation

Your total score provides a general indicator of your current state. Use this as a starting point for discussion, not a definitive judgment.

| Score Range | Interpretation | Focus Area |
| :---: | :--- | :--- |
| **10 - 20** | **Critical Focus:** Significant dissatisfaction or misalignment. Immediate, targeted action is required across multiple dimensions. | Foundational clarity and immediate well-being strategies. |
| **21 - 35** | **Developmental Focus:** Moderate satisfaction, but clear areas for improvement exist. Focus on strengthening specific dimensions. | Targeted skill-building and boundary reinforcement. |
| **36 - 50** | **Optimizing Focus:** High satisfaction and alignment. Focus on maintaining momentum and exploring advanced optimization strategies. | Strategic long-term planning and leadership development. |
