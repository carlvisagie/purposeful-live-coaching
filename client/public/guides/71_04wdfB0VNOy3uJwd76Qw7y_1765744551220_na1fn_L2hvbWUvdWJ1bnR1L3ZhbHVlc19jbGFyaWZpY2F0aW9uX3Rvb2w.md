# Values Clarification Tool: Your Compass for Life and Work

**Category:** Worksheet
**Author:** Manus AI

## Purpose and Instructions

The **Values Clarification Tool** is a foundational exercise designed to help you identify, define, and prioritize the core principles that guide your decisions and behavior. Understanding your values acts as a personal compass, ensuring your actions are aligned with what truly matters to you, leading to greater fulfillment, reduced stress, and more effective goal-setting.

**Instructions:**

1.  **Identify:** Review the provided list of common values and add any others that resonate with you.
2.  **Define:** Write a personal definition for each value—what does it *mean* to you in practical terms?
3.  **Prioritize:** Select your top 10 values and rank them from 1 (most important) to 10 (least important of the top 10).
4.  **Reflect & Act:** Use the reflection questions and action planning section to integrate your values into your daily life.
