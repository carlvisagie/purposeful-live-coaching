# Detox Food Guide

**Module:** Nutrition

**Purpose:** To provide a guide to foods that can help to support your body's natural detoxification processes.

**Instructions:** This guide provides a list of foods that are rich in nutrients that can help to support your liver, kidneys, and digestive system. Incorporate these foods into your diet to help your body to function at its best.

---

### What is Detoxification?

Detoxification is the body's natural process of neutralizing and eliminating toxins. Your liver, kidneys, digestive system, skin, and lungs are all involved in this process. While your body is designed to detoxify itself, you can support this process by eating a healthy diet, getting regular exercise, and reducing your exposure to toxins.

### Tips for a Healthy Detox

*   **Stay hydrated:** Drink plenty of water throughout the day.
*   **Eat a whole-foods diet:** Focus on eating whole, unprocessed foods, such as fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean protein.
*   **Limit your intake of processed foods, sugar, and alcohol:** These foods can put a strain on your body's detoxification systems.
*   **Get regular exercise:** Exercise can help to improve circulation and to promote the elimination of toxins through sweat.
*   **Get enough sleep:** Sleep is essential for your body to repair and regenerate itself.
*   **Reduce your exposure to toxins:** Avoid exposure to environmental toxins, such as pollution, pesticides, and cleaning chemicals.
