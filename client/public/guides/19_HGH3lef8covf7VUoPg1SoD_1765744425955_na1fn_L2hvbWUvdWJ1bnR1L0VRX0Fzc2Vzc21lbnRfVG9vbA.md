# EQ Assessment Tool: Emotional Intelligence Self-Evaluation

## 1. Purpose and Instructions

### Purpose
This **EQ Assessment Tool** is designed to help you understand your emotional intelligence (EQ) across four key domains: **Self-Awareness**, **Self-Regulation**, **Social Awareness (Empathy)**, and **Relationship Management** [1]. Use this self-evaluation to identify specific strengths and areas for targeted personal and professional growth.

### Instructions
1.  Read each statement carefully.
2.  In the "Score (1-5)" column, enter the number that best reflects how often the statement is true for you, using the scale below.
    *   **1 = Never/Rarely**
    *   **2 = Sometimes**
    *   **3 = Often**
    *   **4 = Very Often**
    *   **5 = Always/Consistently**
3.  Sum the scores for each section to get your domain score, and then calculate your Total EQ Score.
