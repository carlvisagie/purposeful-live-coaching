# Risk Assessment Tool: Holistic Well-being Check-in

**Purpose:** To provide a structured, evidence-based method for self-assessing potential risks across key life domains. This tool is designed to help you and your coach identify areas requiring immediate attention, ensuring a safe and effective coaching journey [1].

## Instructions for Use

This tool is a self-assessment and not a substitute for professional medical or psychological evaluation. If you are in immediate crisis, please contact emergency services or a crisis hotline.

1.  **Review:** Read each statement in the **Risk Assessment Matrix** carefully.
2.  **Score:** For each statement, select the score that best reflects your current situation (1 = Low Risk, 5 = High Risk).
3.  **Calculate:** Sum your scores to determine your overall risk level.
4.  **Reflect:** Complete the **Interpretation and Reflection** section.
5.  **Act:** Define your **Action Steps and Safety Plan** with your coach.

## Section 2: Interpretation and Reflection

Your total score provides a general indicator of your current risk profile. Use the table below for guidance, and then answer the reflection questions.

| Total Score Range | Interpretation | Immediate Action Required |
| :---: | :--- | :--- |
| **10 - 18** | **Low Risk:** You are generally stable and resilient. Focus on growth and optimization. | Continue with coaching plan. |
| **19 - 34** | **Moderate Risk:** You have significant challenges in one or more areas. Focus on stabilization and risk mitigation. | Discuss high-scoring domains with your coach immediately. |
| **35 - 50** | **High Risk:** You are experiencing significant distress. **Safety is the priority.** | **Contact your coach and/or emergency contacts immediately.** |
