# Heart Health Assessment

**Category:** Assessment
**Material Name:** Heart Health Assessment

## 1. Purpose and Instructions

### Purpose
This **Heart Health Assessment** is designed to provide a comprehensive, self-guided review of key lifestyle and biometric factors that influence cardiovascular health. Its goal is to help you and your coach identify current strengths, potential risk areas, and establish a clear baseline for your heart health journey. This is not a substitute for medical advice or professional diagnosis. Always consult with a qualified healthcare provider for medical concerns.

### Instructions
1.  Complete all sections honestly and to the best of your knowledge.
2.  Use the provided checkboxes (`[ ]`) to mark items that apply to you.
3.  Fill in the tables with your most recent data.
4.  Answer the reflection questions thoughtfully.
5.  Share the completed assessment with your coach to develop a personalized action plan.
