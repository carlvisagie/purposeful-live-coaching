# Mission Statement Builder: Your North Star Worksheet

**Category:** Worksheet
**Purpose:** To guide you through a structured, evidence-based process to define a clear, compelling, and actionable mission statement that serves as your personal or professional "North Star."

---

## 1. Introduction and Instructions

A powerful mission statement is more than just a catchy phrase; it is a concise declaration of your organization's or your personal purpose. It answers the fundamental question: **"Why do we/I exist?"** A well-crafted mission statement provides focus, aligns actions, and inspires stakeholders [1].

**Instructions for Use:**
1.  Read through the entire worksheet to understand the process.
2.  Complete **Section 2: Core Components** by brainstorming answers to the questions in the table. Do not edit yourself; aim for quantity and clarity.
3.  Use your answers from Section 2 to complete **Section 3: Drafting the Statement**.
4.  Refine your draft using the questions in **Section 4: Reflection and Refinement**.
5.  Commit to your next steps in **Section 5: Action Plan**.

---

## 2. Core Components: The 3 C's Framework

A strong mission statement typically integrates three key elements: **Customers** (Who do you serve?), **Contribution** (What do you do?), and **Competence** (How do you do it uniquely?) [2].
